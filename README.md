## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene where a character sells a seemingly mundane memory that later becomes a viral sensation, exploring the unintended consequences.
- Create a memory-file 'corruption'—a glitch in a recorded experience—and describe its emotional and sensory impact on the user.
- Develop a side story about an 'emotion-dampening therapist' who treats memory sellers, and the ethical dilemmas they face.
- Imagine a black market for 'un-sanitized' historical memories, and a transaction that goes wrong.

### Technical Entry Points

- Design a speculative UI/UX for a 'memory ledger' app that tracks sales, royalties, and emotional toll metrics.
- Outline the architecture of a 'neural purity diagnostic' system that checks memory-files for tampering or embedded metadata.
